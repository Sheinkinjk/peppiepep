# LinkedIn Growth PDF Resources

## Required PDF Files

This directory should contain two high-quality PDF documents for the LinkedIn Growth marketing page:

### 1. creator-partnership-playbook.pdf
**Title:** The Creator Partnership Playbook
**Target Audience:** B2B Marketing & Growth Teams
**Length:** 24+ pages
**Content Requirements:**
- How to build creator partnerships that outperform paid ads
- Step-by-step vetting criteria for selecting the right creators
- Performance benchmarks and ROI calculation templates
- Compliance checklist for FTC and LinkedIn guidelines
- Real campaign examples with actual results
- Payout model structures and examples
- Attribution and tracking best practices
- Template contracts and partnership agreements

**Design Requirements:**
- Professional, ultra-premium design
- Consistent branding with Refer Labs colors (cyan, teal, blue)
- Real data visualizations and charts
- Actionable frameworks and checklists
- High-quality graphics and icons

### 2. linkedin-creator-economics-2025.pdf
**Title:** LinkedIn Creator Economics - Benchmarks & Market Data (2025)
**Target Audience:** B2B Marketers, Growth Leaders, Partnership Managers
**Length:** 16-20 pages
**Content Requirements:**
- 2025 payout benchmarks by industry (SaaS, Fintech, E-commerce, MarTech)
- Creator tier breakdowns (micro, mid-tier, macro creators)
- Conversion rate data: CTR, demo show rates, SQL conversion, close rates
- How to structure competitive commission models
- Case studies from real partnerships
- Market size and growth statistics
- Creator audience demographics and buyer intent data
- ROI calculations and profitability models

**Real Statistics to Include:**
- Average engagement rates on LinkedIn by follower count
- B2B influencer marketing ROI (TopRank Marketing 2024: 89% report ROI)
- LinkedIn active users (1B+ professionals globally)
- B2B buyer research behavior (Gartner: 87% research independently)
- Email open rates (HubSpot 2024: 21.5% average for B2B)
- Creator partnership vs paid ads performance comparison

**Design Requirements:**
- Data-driven visual style with charts and graphs
- Professional typography and layout
- Cited sources for all statistics
- Quarterly update schedule noted
- Downloadable and shareable format
- Print-ready quality (300 DPI minimum)
